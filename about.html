    Our Location

    Ruiru SDA is located in Wataalam, Ruiru Town, Kiambu County, Kenya. We are situated along the Nairobi-Thika Highway, serving both the local community and visitors.

    Our Vision

    To create a self-sustaining welfare system that empowers members to support one another in times of need, fostering a strong, caring Christian community.

    Our Welfare System

    This welfare management system was developed to:

    • Track member contributions transparently
    • Manage welfare funds efficiently
    • Provide easy access to contribution records
    • Support members during emergencies
